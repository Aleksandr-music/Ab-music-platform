Formats

Deliverables are agreed before production. Typical formats:

  • Instrumental tracks (brand, club, lounge, cinematic, sports, etc.)
  • Custom edits (15s / 30s / 60s / full length)

Licensing and pricing depend on usage (platforms, territories, paid ads, exclusivity, term length).
