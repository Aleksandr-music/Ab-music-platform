Lyrics Licensing

Pre-written lyrics from AB Music Platform artist projects — available for licensing

What this is

This is not custom writing. I license selected, already written lyrics from the AB Music Platform catalog (projects shown on this site). You receive a clean, usage-ready text and clear permission terms for your release.

Best for
  • Artists & vocalists preparing a release
  • Producers needing strong lyrics for toplines
  • Labels & indie teams building concept singles/EPs
  • Content projects that require lyrical text on paper (spoken, cinematic, narrative)
How licensing works
  • Catalog-based: you choose from available lyrics (no custom writing).
  • Clear permission: written license terms for usage in your release.
  • Fast delivery: clean text + metadata-ready details (title/credits guidance).
  • Professional workflow: one point of contact and simple approval.

Pricing and royalty structure depend on the intended use (release type, territories, distribution, exclusivity level).
